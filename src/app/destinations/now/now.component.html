Now

The journey that brought you to who you are now could be captured in a timeline as unique as your DNA. The current version of you.

The development and design worlds are filled with patterns and libraries. As they appear and improve over time their version numbers increase.

I like to think of age as a version number. We start at Version 0.0.1 and eventually got to now. It would be fun to have a release log full of bug fixes and new features to look back on.

I'll try to do that here rather than rely on memory.
