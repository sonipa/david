Bulletin Board Systems
A BBS is like a pre-internet website. People would use their modems to call your BBS's phone number. Once connected, they'd see your site.
Many of the features you see in websites existed back then. The person who created and ran the BBS was the “sysop” or system operator.
Wildcat was the program I used to manage my BBS. I was a kid living with my parents so the incoming modem calls were not convenient. The internet is way better.
